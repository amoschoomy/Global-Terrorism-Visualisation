Terrorism in the World

Author: Amos Choo

Below is the world map showing how severe is the act of terrorism in the timespan of 1970-2017. The more saturated the colour at the country is, the worse the terrorism. Countries in the East Asia such as Iraq, India and Afghanistan have been plagued with plentitude of cases of terrorism due to various purposes such as religious motivation or civil war.

The act of terrorism have costed a lot of lives and are still at a dangerously high numbers at around 26000 deaths recorded in total for the year of 2017.
