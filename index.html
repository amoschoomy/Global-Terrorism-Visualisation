Terrorism in the World

Author: Amos Choo

Terrorism has been a reoccuring issue in the world almost every year. Majority of the world has been plagued with multiple terrorist attacks for decades since World War II ended.
Let's take a look at how much terrorism cases has affected each country.

The act of terrorism have costed a lot of lives and are still at a dangerously high numbers at around 26000 deaths recorded in total for the year of 2017.
Although the trend of deaths from terrorism have been quite inconsistent in recent years, the number of deaths due to terrorism have not dropped below 5000 since 2003

An alarming high percentage of 89% was recorded as successful terrorist attack while only 11% of terrorist attacks are foiled.
Authorities needs to take charge to decrease the number of successful terrorist attacks because the lesser the number of succesful terrorist attacks, the lesser the number of casualities recorded

Terrorism comes in many form mostly causing physical harm to humans and/or infrastructures, so here are some of the methodolgies of terrorism studied and visualised.

Bombing is the most common methodology of attack with 88000 of terrorism cases reported are due to bombing.

Even more fascinating, the rest of the terrorism methodology combined only amounts to about 93000 cases which is barely higher than terrorist attacks conducted through bombing.
